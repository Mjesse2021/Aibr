Like else where in the world, insurance brokers are established purposely to mitigate the insured who usually fall victim to their insurers because of being in most times unskilled or less conversant with insurance issues.

However, it should be understood that the commissions to paid to the insurance brokers does not in anyway affect the premium paid by the insured.
