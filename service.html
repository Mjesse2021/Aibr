Business insurance

Alpha insurance Brokers seek to protect your entire business by insuring your equipement that you use everyday,your merchandise, your folders,your business vehicles,loss of income as well as damage to others.

Here are some kind of business insurance that Alpha insurance brokers offer:

Business interruption

Alpha insurance Brokers offers you additional coverage that will allow your business to be insured in the event of an intangible loss following a disaster or a problem(Fire,flood,storm,etc...)

Third party liability

Alpha insurance Brokers covers damages caused to others and which are related to the business, we cover bodily injury and material damages,personal damages or damages attributable to advertising, medical expenses and damages caused to the premises or to the building rented by the business...

Legal counsel coverage

Alpha insurance Brokers insures you by covering the costs of law proceedings and providing you with services in case of dispute between you and a third party.

Key main insurance

Alpha insurance Brokers offers you a liability insurance payable for your directors and officers of your company or to the organisation itself, as indemnification (reimbursement) for looses or advancement of defence costs in the event an insured suffers such a loss as a result of a legal action brought for alleged wrongful acts in their capacity as directors and officers.
